========================================
   3D Maze Game — Controls Reference
========================================

MOVEMENT:
  W  /  Up Arrow      Move forward
  S  /  Down Arrow    Move backward
  A  /  Left Arrow    Strafe left
  D  /  Right Arrow   Strafe right

CAMERA:
  Mouse Movement      Look around (FPS-style)
                      Horizontal = turn left/right
                      Vertical   = look up/down

ACTIONS:
  Space               Fire projectile

TOGGLES:
  Q                   Toggle wireframe / solid mode
  C                   Toggle wall collision on / off
  L                   Toggle lighting on / off
  T                   Toggle textures on / off

SYSTEM:
  ESC                 Exit the game

========================================
   Gameplay
========================================

OBJECTIVE:
  Navigate through the 3D maze and reach
  the golden pulsing cube (the goal).

COLLECTIBLES:
  Pick up the rotating red cubes scattered
  throughout the maze for points.

TIPS:
  - Use the mouse to look around corners
    before moving.
  - Press C to disable collision if you
    get stuck (useful for debugging).
  - Press L to toggle lighting for a
    different visual experience.
  - The golden goal cube pulses and glows
    to help you find it.
